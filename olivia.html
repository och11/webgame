About the Game

What Is Asteroid Dodge?

Asteroid Dodge is a fast-paced, browser-based space survival game built entirely with HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JavaScript. You pilot a spacecraft through an ever-intensifying asteroid field, dodging rocks and blasting them with your laser cannon before they collide with your ship.

The game features an infinite survival loop — the longer you live, the higher your score climbs and the faster and more frequent the asteroids become. Collect golden stars scattered through space for bonus points, and grab shield powerups to gain temporary invincibility. How long can you survive?

Instructions

How to Play

Your goal is simple: don't get hit by asteroids. Survive as long as possible, rack up points, and beat your high score.

MOUSE Move your ship — it follows your cursor ARROW KEYS Alternative movement (Up / Down / Left / Right) SPACEBAR Fire your laser cannon CLICK Also fires the laser cannon ⭐ STARS Fly into gold stars to collect +50 bonus points 🛡 SHIELD Collect the cyan ring for 3 seconds of invincibility

Scoring: Points accumulate automatically over time, and increase faster as your level rises. Shooting large asteroids requires two hits. Every asteroid you destroy awards points equal to its size.
